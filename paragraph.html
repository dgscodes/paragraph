Hachodesh


The Hachodesh reading recounts Hashem's historic communication to Moshe in Egypt on the first of Nissan, two weeks before the Exodus, regarding the establishment of a Jewish (lunar) calendar, the Paschal Offering, matzah, bitter herbs, and the seder.
